\subsection*{Charter}

\textbf{Charter} \emph{(normative)}

This document gives a domain-agnostic, minimal formal specification of Systemics $\\Sigma$. It treats any practice as a kernel-shaped contract that produces decisions from posted evidence under benign variation, with replayable records. No specific domain assumptions (physics, ML, audits, etc.) are required.

\subsection*{Alphabet (Objects \& Maps)}

\textbf{Alphabet} \emph{(normative)}

\begin{itemize}
  \item \(U\): Universe of artifacts.
  \item \(\mathbb{V}\): Valuation space (any measurable space; commonly \textbackslash\{\}mathbb\{R\}\textasciicircum{}k \textbackslash\{\}times \textbackslash\{\}mathbb\{B\}\textasciicircum{}m).
  \item \(\mathbf{2}\): Decision space \textbackslash\{\}mathbf\{2\} = \{0,1\}.
  \item \(\Pi\): Frames (benign contexts).
  \item \(P_n\): Probes (benign perturbations).
  \item \(\Theta\): Floors/thresholds (partially ordered set).
  \item \(\beta\): Invariance budgets (tolerances in a poset/lattice).
  \item \(C\): Capacity budgets (bits/time/energy constraints).
  \item \(\Gamma\): Envelope/meta (versions, seeds, numeric modes, commits).
  \item \(\mathcal{R}\): Records (canonical map \textbackslash\{\}to bytes; hash/ledger optional).
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Definition: Systemic Kernel}

\textbf{Systemic Kernel} \emph{(normative)}

A systemic kernel is the tuple
\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}begin\{equation\}
  K\_\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}mu\textasciicircum{}\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Sigma \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\};=\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}; \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}big(v,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\} \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}chi,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\} \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Pi,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\} P\_n,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\} \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Theta,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\} \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}beta,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\} C,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\} \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Gamma\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}big),
\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}end\{equation\}
where \$v:U\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}to \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}mathbb\{V\}\$ is a valuation map and \$\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}chi:\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}mathbb\{V\}\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}times\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Theta\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}times\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}beta\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}to \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}mathbf\{2\}\$ is a decision gate.

\subsection*{Metrics \& Order}

\textbf{Wobble and orderings} \emph{(normative)}

- A divergence ("wobble") \$w:\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}mathbb\{V\}\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}times\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}mathbb\{V\}\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}to \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}mathbb\{R\}\_\{\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}ge 0\}\$ on decision-relevant coordinates. - Orders: \$\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}theta\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}preceq \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}theta'\$ means tightening floors; \$\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}beta'\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}preceq\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}beta\$ means tightening budgets; \$C'\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}preceq C\$ means shrinking capacity.

\subsection*{Axioms (Minimal Core)}

\textbf{Σ-A1 Well-typedness} \emph{(normative)}

\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}textbf\{(Well-typedness).\} All maps are measurable/continuous as needed; \$\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}chi\$ is total.

\textbf{Σ-A2 Posting / Records-only} \emph{(normative)}

\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}textbf\{(Posting / Records-only).\} For any run on \$u\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}in U\$, the record \$\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}kappa\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}in\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}mathcal\{R\}\$ contains \$(v(u),\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Theta,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}beta,C,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Pi,P\_n,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Gamma)\$, and the decision equals
\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}begin\{equation\}
  \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}chi\textasciicircum{}\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}ast(u;\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}kappa) \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\};=\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}; \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}chi\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}big(v(u),\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Theta,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}beta\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}big),
\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}end\{equation\}
with no dependence on unposted data.

\textbf{Σ-A3 Benign invariance} \emph{(normative)}

\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}textbf\{(Benign invariance).\} Let \$(\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}pi,p)\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}in \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Pi\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}times P\_n\$ act on the measurement/evaluation pathway to yield \$v\_\{\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}pi,p\}(u)\$. Define
\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}begin\{equation\}
  W(u)\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\};:=\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\};\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}sup\_\{(\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}pi,p)\}\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}; w\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}!\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}big(v\_\{\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}pi,p\}(u),\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\},v\_\{\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}pi\_0,p\_0\}(u)\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}big).
\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}end\{equation\}
If \$W(u)\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}preceq \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}beta\$ then \$\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}chi\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}big(v\_\{\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}pi,p\}(u),\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Theta,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}beta\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}big)=\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}chi\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}big(v\_\{\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}pi\_0,p\_0\}(u),\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Theta,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}beta\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}big)\$.

\textbf{Σ-A4 Minimal sufficiency under capacity} \emph{(normative)}

\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}textbf\{(Minimal sufficiency under capacity).\} Among valuations preserving decisions under posted \$(\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Theta,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}beta)\$, \$v\$ is minimal w.r.t.\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\} \$C\$:
\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}begin\{equation\}
  \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}forall v'\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}; \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}big(\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}chi\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}!\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}circ v'=\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}chi\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}!\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}circ v\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}big)\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\} \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Rightarrow\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\} \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}mathrm\{cost\}(v')\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\} \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}succeq\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\} \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}mathrm\{cost\}(v)\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}quad\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}text\{subject to \}C.
\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}end\{equation\}

\textbf{Σ-A5 Reflexive reproducibility} \emph{(normative)}

\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}textbf\{(Reflexive reproducibility).\} There exists an admissible, independently realized \$v'\$ (different numeric/route) such that
\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}begin\{equation\}
  \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}chi\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}big(v(u),\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Theta,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}beta\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}big)\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\};=\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\};\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}chi\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}big(v'(u),\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Theta,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}beta\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}big),
\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}end\{equation\}
with both posted in \$\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}kappa\$ (self-warrant).

\textbf{Σ-A6 Determinism \& idempotence} \emph{(normative)}

\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}textbf\{(Determinism \& idempotence).\} For fixed \$\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}big(v(u),\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Theta,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}beta\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}big)\$, the decision \$\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}chi\$ is unique and idempotent.

\textbf{Σ-A7 Monotonicity} \emph{(normative)}

\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}textbf\{(Monotonicity).\} Tightening floors or budgets cannot rescue a failure by hidden dependence:
\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}begin\{equation\}
  \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}theta\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}preceq \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}theta',\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\} \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}beta'\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}preceq \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}beta\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\};\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Rightarrow\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}
  \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}chi(v,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}theta,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}beta)=1 \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\} \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Rightarrow\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\} \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}chi(v,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}theta',\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}beta')\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}in\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}\{0,1\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}\}\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\} \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}text\{with no hidden rescue.\}
\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}end\{equation\}

\textbf{Σ-A8 Isomorphism invariance} \emph{(normative)}

\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}textbf\{(Isomorphism invariance).\} If a frame \$\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}pi\$ induces a structure-preserving isomorphism on representation, decisions are invariant.

\subsection*{Conformance (Lawful Record)}

\textbf{Σ-lawful record checklist} \emph{(normative)}

A record \$\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}kappa \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}in \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}mathcal\{R\}\$ is \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}emph\{\$\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Sigma\$-lawful\} iff it includes:
1. \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}textbf\{Contract:\} \$\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Theta,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}beta,C,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Pi,P\_n,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Gamma\$ (with any guards like \$\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}epsilon\$). 2. \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}textbf\{Valuation:\} \$v(u)\$ (decision-relevant coordinates posted). 3. \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}textbf\{Decision:\} \$\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}chi\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}big(v(u),\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Theta,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}beta\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}big)\$ and a reason enumerating passed/failed predicates. 4. \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}textbf\{Invariance evidence:\} wobble metrics and the realizing worst-case \$(\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}pi,p)\$. 5. \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}textbf\{Reflexive warrant:\} independent \$v'(u)\$ and agreement of \$\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}chi\$. 6. \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}textbf\{Canonicalization:\} canonical bytes, digest \$d\$, and optional chain root for append-only books.

\subsection*{Morphisms of Systemics}

\textbf{Morphism F: Σ → Σ'} \emph{(normative)}

A morphism \$F:\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Sigma\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}to\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Sigma'\$ is a pair \$(\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}phi\_U,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}phi\_V)\$ with
\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}begin\{equation\}
  v' \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}circ \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}phi\_U \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\};=\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}; \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}phi\_V \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}circ v,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}qquad
  \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}chi' \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}circ (\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\},\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}phi\_V\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}times\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}mathrm\{id\}\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\},) \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\};=\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}; \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}chi,
\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}end\{equation\}
that also maps contracts monotonically: \$F(\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Theta,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}beta,C,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Pi,P\_n,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Gamma)\$ respects the relevant orders and preserves \$(\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}Sigma\$-A1,...,A7).

\textbf{Morphism preservation} \emph{(normative)}

A morphism preserves valuation and decision structure by satisfying:
\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}begin\{equation\}
  v' \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}circ \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}phi\_U \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\};=\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}; \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}phi\_V \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}circ v,\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}qquad
  \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}chi' \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}circ (\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\},\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}phi\_V\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}times\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}mathrm\{id\}\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\},) \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\};=\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}; \textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}chi.
\textbackslash\{\}\textbackslash\{\}end\{equation\}
It also maps contract parameters monotonically and preserves (Σ-A1,...,A7).

\subsection*{Instantiation Recipe (Domain-Agnostic)}

\textbf{Recipe} \emph{(informative)}

To realize $\\Sigma$ in any field: 1. Choose $U$, $\\mathbb{V}$, $v$, $\\chi$. 2. Post $\\Theta,\\beta,C,\\Pi,P_n,\\Gamma$ and a wobble metric $w$. 3. Establish (Σ-A1,...,A7) by construction and tests. 4. Emit lawful $\\kappa$ and (optionally) hash-chain pages into a book.

\subsection*{Notes}

\textbf{Notes} \emph{(informative)}

This specification does not fix what $v$ measures, what $\\chi$ decides, or how $w$ is computed. It only requires posting, invariance under benign variation, minimal sufficiency under capacity, and reflexive reproducibility. Evidence Systemics is one instantiation where $v$ encodes evidence gauges; other instances (Control, Protocol, Risk, Learning, etc.) keep the same $\\Sigma$-contract while choosing different $v,\\Theta,\\beta$.
